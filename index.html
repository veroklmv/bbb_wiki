B U I L D

Build Jakapan Puttha (บิว จักรพันธ์ พุทธา) is a thai model and actor. He was born on the 4th of June, 1994. He graduated from Bangkok University with a Broadcasting bachelor degree.

Before starring in TV shows, Build was part of a music group called Stickwithfriends. He was active in it during 2015 and 2016.

He played in various series as Yeua Payabaat (2017, Tra Barb See Chompoo (2018), Praomook (2021), KinnPorsche (2022). The latter is his most popular one, in which he plays the role of Pete, the head bodyguard of the main family.

B I B L E

Bible Wichapas Sumettikul (วิชญ์ภาส สุเมตติกุล) is a thai model and actor. Hewas born on the 25th of December, 1997. He graduated from Thammasat University with a Mechanical Engineering bachelor degree. He is bilingual and speaks thai and english.

Kinnporsche is his first series, even though he tried to audition for other roles in the past.

In Kinnporsche, he plays the role of Vegas, the heir of the minor family.
